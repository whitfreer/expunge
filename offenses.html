List of Serious Juvenile Offenses

  • Illegal Manufacture, Distribution, Sale, Prescription, Dispensing , Administration of a Controlled Substance
  • Illegal Sale, Delivery or transfer of Pistols/Revolvers  and Making a False Statement in Regard to the Purchase, Sale Delivery or Other Transfer of a Pistol/Revolver
  • Carrying a Gun Without a Permit
  • Injury or Risk Of Injury to or Impairing Morals of Children,  Sale of Children
  • Manufacture of Bombs
  • Sale or Transfer of Assault Weapon; Possession of Assault Weapon
  • Extortionate Extension of Credit
  • Murder
  • Manslaughter
  • Misconduct With a Motor Vehicle
  • Assault in the 1st or 2nd Degree And With a Firearm
  • Assault of Elderly, Blind, Disabled, Pregnant, Intellectually Disabled in the 1st or 2nd Degree  And With a Firearm
  • Assault of a Correction Officer
  • Assault of Public Safety, Emergency Medical or Public Transit Personnel
  • Strangulation in the 1st or 2nd Degree
  • Sexual Assault and Aggravated Sexual Assault in the 1st and 2nd Degree
  • Sexual Assault in Spousal or Cohabiting Relationship
  • Aggravated Sexual Assault of a Minor
  • Sexual Assault in the 3rd Degree With a Firearm
  • Promoting Prostitution
  • Kidnapping in the 1st or 2nd Degree And With a Firearm
  • Unlawful Restraint
  • Home Invasion
  • Burglary in the 1st Degree  and 2nd and 3rd Degree With a Firearm
  • Arson in the 1st ,  2nd and 3rd Degree
  • Larceny in the 1st degree by Extortion; Larceny  in the 2nd Degree
  • Robbery in the 1st  and 2nd Degree And Involving An Occupied Motor Vehicle
  • Unauthorized Conveyance of Items Into Correctional Institution
  • Employing a Minor in an Obscene Performance
  • Possession of a Sawed-Off Shotgun or Silencer
  • Stealing a Firearm
  • Criminal Use of a Firearm or Electronic Defense Weapon
  • Possession of a Weapon on School Grounds
