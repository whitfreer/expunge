Face Forward Program


Face Forward 3 offers free employment and training services (including erasure of juvenile records) for youth who have been involved with the juvenile justice system, are between 14 and 24 and live in the following towns:


Ansonia
Beacon Falls
Bridgeport
Darien
Derby
Easton
Fairfield
Greenwich
Monroe
New Canaan
Norwalk
Oxford
Seymour
Shelton
Stamford
Stratford
Trumbull
Weston
Westport
Wilton

Face Forward 3 Services Include:

  • Creating & implementing an individual career plan with appropriate education and training goals
  • Occupational skills training in demand industries leading to industry recognized credentials
  • Creation of an electronic portfolio to store career guidance and labor market information
  • Workforce activities which lead to employment in construction, healthcare or transportation industries
  • Educational interventions including high school completion, tutoring and or ABE/GED preparation
  • Comprehensive case management services, including family supports
  • Supportive coaching from a mentor
  • Service learning projects to build workforce development skills
  • Legal Services to complete expungement and/or erasure of juvenile records when eligible
  • Follow up services for 9 months (which may include: resolution of problems in school or work, assistance in securing better paying employment, furthering education, leadership development, etc.)
